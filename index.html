About NutraPrep

NutraPrep is an app designed to help you with your food prep needs. You'll be able to search up foods and recipes to find out their nutritional value. You'll also be able to look for recipes by a specific protein you may have on hand, (like chicken for example), or you can look for a recipe that fits a dietary need with the categories provided.

About the Nutrition Tab:
Search for a food and find out its nutritional value! You'll find information about it's amount of calories, fats, sugars and proteins.
About the recipes Tab:
This is where you'll be able to search for a recipe by an ingredient or by one of the categories provided. You'll be able to look up a recipe that is: low carb, low sodium, low calorie, low sugar or high in protein.
